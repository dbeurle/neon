void neon::Hexahedron8::precompute_shape_functions ( )
protected

Initialize the shape functions to the following polynomials

\begin{align*} N_1(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1-\xi)(1-\eta)(1-\zeta) \\ N_2(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1+\xi)(1-\eta)(1-\zeta) \\ N_3(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1+\xi)(1+\eta)(1-\zeta) \\ N_4(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1-\xi)(1+\eta)(1-\zeta) \\ N_5(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1-\xi)(1-\eta)(1+\zeta) \\ N_6(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1+\xi)(1-\eta)(1+\zeta) \\ N_7(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1+\xi)(1+\eta)(1+\zeta) \\ N_8(\xi, \eta, \zeta) &= \frac{1}{8}(1-\xi)(1+\eta)(1+\zeta) \end{align*}
